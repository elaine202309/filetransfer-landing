Features

Chat-Style Transfer

Send messages and files just like texting. Zero learning curve. Text arrives instantly, files come with previews and one-click downloads.

Files sent from PC are automatically saved to the Files app on your iPhone. No manual saving — receive and use immediately.

Direct device-to-device transfer over the same WiFi. Your files never pass through third-party servers — fast and private.
